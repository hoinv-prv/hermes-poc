## 3. Canonical decisions

1. Practical name: **Source Understanding Artifact**.
2. Full descriptive name: **Source-derived Reusable Understanding Artifact**.
3. One artifact should correspond to one clear source unit.
4. Minimum provenance: source pointer, source scope, understanding/extraction date.
5. Lightweight status/authority/freshness hints are required.
6. Raw/source remains source of truth.
7. Source Understanding Artifact may be working/reference, capture candidate, or curated Knowledge Hub content.
8. Controlled capture/review is required before curated Knowledge Hub status.
9. Wiki Meta / Index may route to Source Understanding Artifact.
10. Artifact may reduce unnecessary raw/source reopen, but raw/source verification remains required when exactness/evidence/freshness matters.
